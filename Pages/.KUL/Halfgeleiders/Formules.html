Concentration

$$ \begin{split} n &= N_c \exp\left(\frac{E_f - E_c}{kT}\right) \\ p &= N_v \exp\left(\frac{E_v - E_f}{kT}\right) \\ \end{split} $$ $$ \begin{split} N_c &= 2 \left(\frac{2\pi m_{de} kT}{h^2}\right)^{3/2} M_c \\ N_v &= 2 \left(\frac{2\pi m_{dh} kT}{h^2}\right)^{3/2} \\ \end{split} $$

Pure Semiconductor in Equilibrium

$$ \begin{split} n &= p = n_i \\ n_i^2 &= np = N_c N_v \exp\left(\frac{-E_g}{kT}\right) \\ E_f &= E_i = \frac{E_c+E_v}{2} + \frac{kT}{2} \ln\left(\frac{N_v}{N_c}\right) \\ \end{split} $$

Doped Semiconductor in Equilibrium

Charge neutrality: $$ N_D + p = N_A + n $$ N-type $$ \begin{split} N_D &\gt N_A \\ n &= \frac{1}{2}\left(N_D - N_A + \sqrt{\left(N_D - N_A\right)^2 + 4 n_i^2}\right) \\ &\approx N_D - N_A \\ p &= \frac{n_i^2}{n} \\ &\approx \frac{n_i^2}{N_D - N_A} \\ E_f &\approx E_i + kT \ln\left(\frac{N_D - N_A}{n_i}\right) \gt E_i \\ \end{split} $$ P-type $$ \begin{split} N_A &\gt N_D \\ p &= \frac{1}{2}\left(N_A - N_D + \sqrt{\left(N_A - N_D\right)^2 + 4 n_i^2}\right) \\ &\approx N_A - N_D \\ n &= \frac{n_i^2}{p} \\ &\approx \frac{n_i^2}{N_A - N_D} \\ E_f &\approx E_i - kT \ln\left(\frac{N_A - N_D}{n_i}\right) \lt E_i \\ \end{split} $$

Semiconductor out of Equilibrium

$$ pn \gt n_i^2 $$ $$ \begin{split} n &= N_c \exp\left(\frac{E_{fn} - E_c}{kT}\right) \\ &= n_i \exp\left(\frac{E_{fn} - E_i}{kT}\right) \\ p &= N_v \exp\left(\frac{E_v - E_{fp}}{kT}\right) \\ &= n_i \exp\left(\frac{E_i - E_{fp}}{kT}\right) \\ np &= N_c N_v \exp\left(\frac{(E_v - E_c) + (E_{fn} - E_{fp})}{kT}\right) \\ &= n_i^2 \exp\left(\frac{E_{fn} - E_{fp}}{kT}\right) \\ \end{split} $$

Carrier Recombination and Generation Mechanisms

$$ \begin{split} U_n \approx&\frac{n-n_{po}}{\tau_n} \\ U_p \approx&\frac{p-p_{no}}{\tau_p} \\ \frac{dn}{dt} &= \frac{dp}{dt} = -U + G = G_{th} + G - R \\ \end{split} $$

Radiative generation and recombination

Usually negligible for indirect semiconductors $$ R_r = B n p $$ N-type $$ \begin{split} R_r &= B p n_0 = \frac{p}{\tau_p} \\ \tau_p &= \frac{1}{B n_0} \approx \frac{1}{B N_D} \\ U_r &= \frac{p-p_0}{\tau_p} \\ \end{split} $$ P-type $$ \begin{split} R_r &= B n p_0 = \frac{n}{\tau_n} \\ \tau_n &= \frac{1}{B p_0} \approx \frac{1}{B N_A} \\ U_r &= \frac{n-n_0}{\tau_n} \\ \end{split} $$ Radiative generation $$ \alpha = \left(h\nu - E_g\right)^\gamma \quad \left(h\nu \gt E_g\right) $$

Auger recombination and Impact Ionization

Auger recombination: Energy released by band-to-band recombination is given to another free hole or electron $$ \begin{split} R_A &= G_{An} n^2 p + G_{Ap} p^2 n \\ R_A &\approx G_{An} N_D^2 p = \frac{p}{\tau_p} \quad \text{n-type} \\ R_A &\approx G_{Ap} N_A^2 n = \frac{n}{\tau_n} \quad \text{p-type} \\ \end{split} $$ Impact ionization: Kinetic energy of an electorn or hole is released in a collision to a neutral atom and generates an electron-hole pair $$ \begin{split} G_i &= \alpha_n n v_n + \alpha_p p v_p \\ \alpha_n(\varepsilon) &= \frac{q\varepsilon}{E_{i,n}} \exp\left(-\frac{b}{\varepsilon}\right) \\ \alpha_p(\varepsilon) &= \frac{q\varepsilon}{E_{i,p}} \exp\left(-\frac{b}{\varepsilon}\right) \\ \end{split} $$

Effective minority carrier lifetime

$$ \frac{1}{\tau} = \frac{1}{\tau_{\text{SRH}}} + \frac{1}{\tau_{\text{rad}}} + \frac{1}{\tau_{\text{Auger}}} $$

Carrier Transport

$$ \begin{split} \varepsilon &= \frac{1}{q} \nabla E_i \\ \varepsilon &= -\nabla \Psi \\ \Psi &= -\frac{E_i}{q} \end{split} $$ $$ \phi_f = -\frac{E_f}{q} $$ Equilibrium: $$ \begin{split} n &= n_i \exp\left(\frac{\Psi - \phi_f}{V_t}\right) \\ p &= n_i \exp\left(\frac{\phi_f - \Psi}{V_t}\right) \\ \end{split} $$ Out of equilibrium: $$ \begin{split} n &= n_i \exp\left(\frac{\Psi - \phi_{fn}}{V_t}\right) \\ p &= n_i \exp\left(\frac{\phi_{fp} - \Psi}{V_t}\right) \\ p n &= n_i^2 \exp\left(\frac{\phi_{fp} - \phi_{fn}}{V_t}\right) \end{split} $$

Diffusion

$$ \begin{split} J_{\text{diffusion},n} &= -q D_n \left(-\nabla n\right) \\ J_{\text{diffusion},p} &= q D_p \left(-\nabla p\right) \\ D_n &= \mu_n \frac{kT}{q} \\ D_p &= \mu_p \frac{kT}{q} \\ \end{split} $$

Current equations

$$ \begin{split} J_n &= q \mu_n \left(n \varepsilon + \frac{kT}{q} \nabla n\right) &= -q \mu_n n \nabla \phi_{fn} \\ J_p &= q \mu_p \left(p \varepsilon - \frac{kT}{q} \nabla p\right) &= -q \mu_p p \nabla \phi_{fp} \\ \end{split} $$
