Detailed Description

A class for changing the setting of a Bank depending on the selection of a Selector.

Can be used for selecting fader banks, changing the channel of MIDI_Control_Elements, transposing notes, displaying different tracks for MIDIInputElements, etc.

Refer to the documentation of Selector for information on the different modes of operation.
