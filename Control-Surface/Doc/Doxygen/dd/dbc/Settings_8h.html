typedef int16_t ANALOG_FILTER_TYPE

The signed integer type to use for analog inputs during filtering. Should be at least \( 10+1+2·ANALOG\_FILTER\_SHIFT\_FACTOR \) bits wide. (10 bits of ADC resolution, 1 sign bit, and 2·ANALOG_FILTER_SHIFT_FACTOR bits of fixed point precision for intermediate filter calculations.
